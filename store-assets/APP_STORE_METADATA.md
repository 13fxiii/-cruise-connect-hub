## FULL DESCRIPTION

Cruise Connect Hub is where Naija culture lives online. Whether you're here to vibe, connect, learn, or earn — this is your bus. 🚌💛

**LIVE SPACES 🎙️**
Join live audio rooms hosted by the community. Music debates, motivation, comedy, gist — it's always on.

**GAMES HUB 🎮**
Play Trivia, Ludo, Naija Wordle, Spin the Wheel, and more. Win real Naira prizes in tournaments.

**CC WALLET 💰**
Earn points from every activity. Convert to real Naira, send gifts to friends, and withdraw directly to your bank.

**MUSIC HUB 🎵**
Discover and stream Naija artists across Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and Audiomack — all in one place.

**MOVIE HUB 🎬**
Nollywood spotlight, watch parties, and community film recommendations.

**JOBS BOARD 💼**
Nigeria-focused job listings for creatives, tech talent, and entrepreneurs.

**C&C SHOP 🛍️**
Community merch and vendor marketplace. Buy, sell, and support Nigerian businesses.

**PR / ADS 📢**
Promote your brand to 3,000+ active community members.

Whether you're vibing on a Tuesday night or grinding toward your bag — Cruise Connect Hub has a seat for you.

We dey Cruise. We dey Connect. We dey Grow. 🚀
